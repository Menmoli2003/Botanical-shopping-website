Some common questions

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Most flower drops happen due to a lack of pollination. You can notice this, especially in blooms. If some buds around your garden are blooming and others fall off, it’s because they didn’t get pollinated.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Moreover, stems and leaves become pale in color, usually white or yellow. The lack of chlorophyll causes the dark-grown plant turn pale in color. Chlorophyll is the pigment in leaves that makes them green. A condition in which leaves produce insufficient chlorophyll is called chlorosis.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Plant leaves turn brown and crispy for a variety of reasons. Toxic water, PH, Salt accumulation in soil, humidity issues, fertilizers and watering practices can all cause brown leaves on plants. This post will cover them all and show you how to fix them. Reasons Plants get Brown Crispy Leaves. Leaf browning can occur for a variety of reasons.

How do i choose a plant?

Choosing Plants Based on Garden Conditions Work with the space you have available. Plant large and fast-growing specimens if you have lots of space. Try container gardening if ground space is limited. Select plants that are suited to the soil at your growing site. Observe the light levels in your garden.

How do I change the pots?

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

Gnats are tiny flying insects with a lifespan of four months. The primary reason that causes gnats to appear is over-watering houseplants. Because gnats feed on decayed plant parts, it’s best to prune your plants regularly. If you wish to get rid of gnats naturally, try using apple cider vinegar or soil covers.
